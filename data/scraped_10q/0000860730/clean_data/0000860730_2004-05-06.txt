The terms  HCA  or the  Company,  as used in this Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q, refer to HCA Inc. and its affiliates unless otherwise stated or indicated by context. First Quarter 2004 Operations Summary       Increases in uninsured patient volumes continue to provide a significant challenge for HCA. On a same facility basis, uninsured admissions increased 13.7% for the first quarter of 2004 compared to the first quarter of 2003.
