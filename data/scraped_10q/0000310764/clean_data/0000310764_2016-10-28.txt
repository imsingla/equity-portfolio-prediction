Foreign currency exchange contracts and interest rate hedges are included in Level 2, as we use inputs other than quoted prices that are observable for the asset or liability. For certain Level 3 liabilities, the Black-Scholes option pricing model was used to value the liabilities, while the fair value of other liabilities was estimated using a discounted cash flow technique. Significant unobservable inputs to this technique included our probability assessments related to the occurrence of certain events, appropriately discounted considering the uncertainties associated with the obligation. We remeasure the fair value of our assets and liabilities each reporting period.
