9.   Collaboration Agreement – Novartis Pharma AG
 
    In March 2003, the Company entered into a collaboration agreement with Novartis Pharma AG (“Novartis”) to jointly develop and commercialize the Company’s Interleukin-1 Trap (“IL-1 Trap”).
