As a basis for considering market participant assumptions in fair value measurements, ASC Topic 820 establishes a fair value hierarchy, which prioritizes the inputs used in measuring fair values as follows:Level 1:Observable inputs such as quoted prices in active markets;Level 2:Inputs, other than quoted prices in active markets, that are observable either directly or indirectly; andLevel 3:Unobservable inputs in which there is little or no market data, which require the reporting entity to develop its own assumptions.Assets and liabilities measured at fair value are based on one or assets or liabilities.B.Cost approach: Amount that would be required to replace the service capacity of an asset (replacement cost).C.Income approach: Techniques to convert future amounts to a single Options are only dilutive when the average market price of the underlying common stock exceeds the exercise price of the options.
