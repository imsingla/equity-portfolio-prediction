Item 2   Management s Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations Financial Overview       Total System Services, Inc. s (TSYS  or the Company s) revenues are derived from providing electronic payment processing and related services to financial and nonfinancial institutions, generally under long-term processing contracts. TSYS  services are provided primarily through the Company s cardholder systems, TS2 and TS1, to financial institutions and other organizations throughout the United States and internationally. The Company currently offers merchant acquiring services to financial institutions and other organizations mainly through its majority owned subsidiary, GP Network Corporation (GP Net), and its wholly owned subsidiary, TSYS Acquiring Solutions, L.L.C. (TSYS Acquiring).       Due to the somewhat seasonal nature of the credit card industry, TSYS  revenues and results of operations have generally increased in the fourth quarter of each year because of increased transaction and authorization volumes during the traditional holiday shopping season.
