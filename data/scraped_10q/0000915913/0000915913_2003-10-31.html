         Third Quarter 2003 Compared with Third Quarter 2002

The rising cost of raw materials and energy continued this quarter to limit the Company’s ability to generate higher quarter-to-quarter earnings, amounting to about $17 million in additional costs that we are unable to pass on to our customers.

The natural gas contracts qualify as cash flow hedges under SFAS 133 and are marked to market. Capital Stock
